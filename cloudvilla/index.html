Perched at 2,400 metres above sea level in the heart of the Swiss Alps, Nuage Estate offers an unparalleled private retreat for those who seek seclusion, grandeur, and a profound connection with the natural world.

The estate encompasses a fully staffed 8-bedroom residence, private ski access, a heated outdoor infinity pool overlooking three valleys, and a spa that rivals the world's finest. Every last detail has been designed with obsessive care.

Architecture

Conceived by Pritzker laureate Jean-Marc Pellerin, the main house is a dialogue between raw Alpine stone and floor-to-ceiling glass — respecting the mountain's permanence while embracing its panoramic, ever-shifting light.

Interiors

Curated over two decades by the Maison Artefact team in Brussels, the interiors blend museum-quality art with handwoven textiles, reclaimed Alpine timber, and bespoke furniture. Each of the eight suites is a world unto itself.

The Grounds

Twelve hectares of private land include manicured gardens, an outdoor heated infinity pool, a cedar-clad spa pavilion, two groomed private ski runs, and a helipad for seamless arrival from Geneva or Zurich.

Setting & Access

Above Zermatt,
Switzerland.

A World Apart

Nuage Estate sits at 2,400 metres in the Mattertal valley with direct sight lines to the Matterhorn. The location combines near-absolute seclusion with discreet proximity to Zermatt's village and its constellation of Michelin-starred dining.

  • 35 minutes by private helicopter from Geneva International
  • 8 minutes to Zermatt village via private cable car
  • Direct access to 360 km of ski pistes
  • Three Michelin-starred restaurants within 15 minutes
  • Private snow groomer for two on-estate ski runs
